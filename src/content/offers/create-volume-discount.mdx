# Create a Volume Discount

Create a volume discount offer that rewards customers with bigger savings when they buy more. Set up tiered pricing with percentage, fixed amount, or fixed price discounts.

<Callout type="info">
  **Prerequisites:** AOV Bundle installed. At least one product in your store.
</Callout>

## Overview

A Volume Discount offer displays a pricing table on your product pages showing customers how much they save at each quantity tier. For example: "Buy 2, save 10%. Buy 3, save 20%."

## Steps

<Steps>

### Step 1: Create a new Volume Discount offer

Navigate to **Offers** in the sidebar, click **Create new offer**, then click **Create** on the **Volume Discount** card.

![Create Volume Discount](/images/create-volume-discount/step-1.png)

### Step 2: Enter the offer name

In the **Offer Info** section, enter a name for your offer in the **Offer name** field. This name is for your internal reference — customers won't see it.

![Enter offer name](/images/create-volume-discount/step-2.png)

### Step 3: Set trigger conditions

In the **Trigger Conditions** section, choose which products will trigger this offer. Select specific products or entire collections. You can also exclude certain products or collections.

![Set trigger conditions](/images/create-volume-discount/step-3.png)

### Step 4: Choose the discount type

In the **Discount Tier** section, select the **Discount type** from the dropdown:
- **% Discount** — Percentage off the price
- **$ Discount** — Fixed amount off the price
- **Fixed Price** — Set a specific price (available when triggered by specific product)

![Choose discount type](/images/create-volume-discount/step-4.png)

### Step 5: Configure discount tiers

Click on the tier buttons (Tier 1, Tier 2, etc.) to configure each tier. For each tier, set:
- **Product quantity** — Minimum items to qualify
- **Discount value** — The discount amount
- **Title** — Display text (e.g., "Buy 2 & Save 10%")
- **Subtitle** — Additional description

Click **Add Tier** to add more tiers. You can also click **AI Suggestion** to auto-generate tier values.

![Configure discount tiers](/images/create-volume-discount/step-5.png)

### Step 6: Add a gift product (optional)

Enable the **Add Gift** toggle to include a free or discounted gift when customers reach a tier. Select the gift product, set the gift discount type, and configure the gift quantity.

![Add gift product](/images/create-volume-discount/step-6.png)

### Step 7: Configure display settings

Click **Next: Display setting** to customize the offer widget appearance. Choose colors, layout (vertical or horizontal), and text styling to match your store's theme.

![Display settings](/images/create-volume-discount/step-7.png)

### Step 8: View your offer in the list

Your offer is automatically saved as a **Draft**. Click **Offer** in the sidebar to return to the offer list. To make it live, toggle the status to **Active** using the play icon in the Actions column.

![Save the offer](/images/create-volume-discount/step-8.png)

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  You can set a **Schedule** for your offer to automatically activate and deactivate at specific dates. Expand the **Schedule** section before saving to configure this.
</Callout>
