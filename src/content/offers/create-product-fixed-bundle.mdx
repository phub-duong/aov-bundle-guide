# Create a Product Fixed Bundle

Create a pre-made bundle of fixed products sold together at a discounted price. Supports up to 30 products and 2,048 variants per bundle with configurable pricing per variant.

<Callout type="info">
  **Prerequisites:** AOV Bundle installed. Products you want to bundle should already exist in your store.
</Callout>

## Overview

A Product Fixed Bundle appears as a single product listing on your store with all included items shown. Customers see the original price crossed out alongside the bundled discount price. You control which variants are available and the displayed price for each.

## Steps

<Steps>

### Step 1: Create a new Product Fixed Bundle offer

Navigate to **Offers** → **Create new offer**, then click **Create** on the **Product Fixed Bundle** card.

![Create Product Fixed Bundle](/images/create-product-fixed-bundle/step-1.png)

### Step 2: Enter the offer title

In the **Offer Information** section, enter a title for your bundle. This title will be visible to customers on your store.

![Enter offer title](/images/create-product-fixed-bundle/step-2.png)

### Step 3: Select bundle products

In the **Product Display** section, use the product browser to select the products that make up this bundle. You can add up to 30 products. The sidebar summary shows your current count (e.g., "5/30 bundled products").

![Select bundle products](/images/create-product-fixed-bundle/step-3.png)

### Step 4: Set applicable products

In the **Applicable Products** section, choose which product pages will display this bundle offer. This determines where on your store the bundle will be shown.

![Set applicable products](/images/create-product-fixed-bundle/step-4.png)

### Step 5: Configure pricing

In the **Offer Details** section, select a **Discount method** and configure the pricing. A table displays each variant with its **Original Price** and **Displayed price** so you can see the exact savings customers will receive.

![Configure pricing](/images/create-product-fixed-bundle/step-5.png)

### Step 6: Set the bundle status

In the sidebar, use the **Bundle Status** section to set the offer to **Active** or **Draft**. Active bundles are immediately visible on your store.

![Set bundle status](/images/create-product-fixed-bundle/step-6.png)

### Step 7: Save the bundle

Click **Save** to create the Product Fixed Bundle. Once saved, you can preview it by clicking **Preview on online store** or **View in Shopify**.

![Save the bundle](/images/create-product-fixed-bundle/step-7.png)

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  Product Fixed Bundles support a maximum of 30 products and 2,048 variants. The sidebar summary displays your current usage against these limits.
</Callout>
