# Customize Widget Appearance

Customize how your bundle offer widgets look on your storefront. Adjust colors, layout, fonts, button styles, and content text to match your store's brand identity.

<Callout type="info">
  **Prerequisites:** AOV Bundle installed with at least one offer created.
</Callout>

## Overview

The design editor uses a three-panel layout: settings form on the left, live preview in the center (with desktop/mobile toggle), and theme/color options on the right.

## Steps

<Steps>

### Step 1: Open the design editor

Click **Widget Display** in the left sidebar, then click on the design type you want to customize (e.g., **Volume Table**, **FBT**, **Bundle Builder**).

![Select design type](/images/customize-widget/step-1.png)

### Step 2: Choose a color style

In the right panel, choose between **Basic** (pre-built themes) and **Advanced** (custom colors):
- **Basic** — Click any pre-built theme card to instantly apply its color scheme
- **Advanced** — Use individual color pickers to customize each UI element

![Choose color style](/images/customize-widget/step-2.png)

### Step 3: Customize colors (Advanced mode)

In Advanced mode, expand each color group to customize:
- **General** — Primary and secondary colors
- **Product Card** — Borders, backgrounds, selected states
- **Button** — Background and text colors
- **Badge** — Badge and tag colors
- **Message** — Alert and notification colors

Click any color swatch to open the color picker and choose your custom color.

![Customize colors](/images/customize-widget/step-3.png)

### Step 4: Edit content text

Switch to the **Content Settings** tab in the left panel. Here you can customize the text displayed on the widget:
- **Add to Cart** button label
- **Item Added** confirmation message
- **See More** / **See Less** toggle text
- **Savings display** text format
- **Stock validation** warning message

![Edit content text](/images/customize-widget/step-4.png)

### Step 5: Choose a layout option

For design types that support multiple layouts (like Volume Table), select between layout options:
- **Vertical** — Stacked tier rows
- **Horizontal** — Side-by-side tier columns
- **Amazon-style** — Grid layout similar to Amazon

![Choose layout](/images/customize-widget/step-5.png)

### Step 6: Preview on desktop and mobile

Use the preview toggle in the center panel to switch between **Desktop** and **Mobile** views. Verify your widget looks good on both screen sizes.

![Preview desktop and mobile](/images/customize-widget/step-6.png)

### Step 7: Save your design

Click **Save** in the top bar to apply your changes. A confirmation message will appear when your design is saved successfully.

![Save design](/images/customize-widget/step-7.png)

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  If you leave the design editor without saving, a **Discard** confirmation will appear. Make sure to save your changes before navigating away.
</Callout>
