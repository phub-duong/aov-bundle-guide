# Install AOV.ai Bundle

Getting started takes less than a minute! Here's how to install AOV.ai Bundle on your Shopify store.

## Steps

### Step 1: Find the app

Go to the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/) and search for **"AOV.ai Bundle"**, or navigate to **Apps** in your Shopify admin sidebar and click **Shopify App Store**.

![Search for AOV Bundle](/.gitbook/assets/install-app/step-1.png)

### Step 2: Add the app

Click the **Add app** button on the AOV.ai Bundle listing page.

![Click Add app](/.gitbook/assets/install-app/step-2.png)

### Step 3: Approve and install

Review the permissions, then click **Install app** to confirm.

![Approve and install](/.gitbook/assets/install-app/step-3.png)

### Step 4: You're in!

After installation, you'll land on the AOV Bundle dashboard with a welcome greeting and the **Setup guide** to help you get started.

![AOV Bundle dashboard](/.gitbook/assets/install-app/step-4.png)

> **Tip:** If the app doesn't appear, try refreshing your Shopify admin page. The app will show up under **Apps** in your sidebar.
