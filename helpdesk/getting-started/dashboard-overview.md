# Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is your home base in AOV.ai Bundle. Here's a quick tour of what you'll find.

## Navigation sidebar

The left sidebar is your main menu. Click any item to jump between sections:

* **Dashboard** — Home overview with metrics and setup guide
* **Offer** — Create and manage all your bundle offers
* **Widget display** — Customize how offers look on your storefront
* **Free gift** — Set up free gift campaigns
* **Analytics** — Track revenue, conversions, and offer performance

![Navigation sidebar](/.gitbook/assets/dashboard-overview/step-1.png)

## Setup guide

The **Setup guide** walks you through the essential steps to get AOV.ai Bundle running:

1. ✅ **Enable app** — Activate the app embed on your theme
2. ✅ **Create your first offer** — Launch your first bundle promotion
3. 🔲 **Visualize how it appears on your store** — Preview the widget on your storefront
4. 🔲 **Get better results** — Optimize your offers for maximum revenue

![Setup guide](/.gitbook/assets/dashboard-overview/step-2.png)

## Overview metrics

Below the setup guide, the **Overview** section displays your key performance numbers at a glance:

* **Additional revenue** — Total extra revenue generated by bundle offers
* **Revenue generated by AOV** — Revenue attributed to AOV.ai Bundle
* **Orders generated by AOV** — Number of orders that include a bundle
* **AOV increase** — How much your average order value has grown

![Overview metrics](/.gitbook/assets/dashboard-overview/step-3.png)

## App block status

Check whether the AOV.ai Bundle **app embed** is active on your theme. If it shows **Active**, the bundle widgets will display on your storefront.

![App block status](/.gitbook/assets/dashboard-overview/step-4.png)
