# Create a Frequently Bought Together Offer

Frequently Bought Together (FBT) lets you create a set of products that are commonly purchased together and offer them as a bundle with a discount. The widget appears on your product pages, encouraging customers to add complementary items to their cart in one click.

> **Info:** **Prerequisites:** AOV Bundle installed and active. Products must already be added to your Shopify store. Optionally, enable the app embed block in your theme settings for the widget to display on your storefront.

## Why Frequently Bought Together?

- **Increase average order value** — By suggesting related products at the point of purchase, customers are more likely to add extra items to their order.
- **Improve shopping experience** — Customers discover products that complement what they are already buying, saving them time browsing your store.
- **AI-powered recommendations** — Let the app automatically suggest trending product combinations based on your store data, so you don't have to guess what pairs well.

## Steps

1. Go to **AOV.ai Bundle Discounts > Offers**.
2. Click **Create offer**.
3. Select **Frequently Bought Together** from the offer type list.
4. Click **Create** to proceed.

![Select Frequently Bought Together offer type](/.gitbook/assets/create-fbt/step-1.png)

5. In the **Trigger conditions** section, choose **Any items from**:
   - **Specific products** — Select individual products that will show the FBT widget.
   - **Specific collections** — Choose entire collections to apply the widget to all products in those collections.
6. Click **Browse** to search and select your trigger products or collections.
7. (Optional) Use **Exclude products** or **Exclude collections** to remove specific items from the trigger.

![Configure trigger conditions](/.gitbook/assets/create-fbt/step-2.png)

8. In the **Suggested products** section, click one of the product suggestion methods:
   - **AI recommendation** — The app suggests trending products automatically.
   - **Same collection** — Products from the same collection as the trigger product.
   - **Specific product** — Manually pick individual products.
   - **Specific collection** — Pick products from a specific collection.
9. Add 2 or more products to create a meaningful bundle.
10. (Optional) Click **Add free gift** to attach a gift product when customers buy all items.

![Select bundle products](/.gitbook/assets/create-fbt/step-3.png)

11. In the **Discount settings** section, select a **Discount type**:
    - **Percentage** — Discount a percentage off the total (e.g., 20% off).
    - **Fixed amount** — Discount a specific dollar amount (e.g., $10 off).
    - **Fixed price** — Set a fixed total price for the bundle.
    - **Free** — Make one of the items free.
12. Enter the **Value** for your discount.
13. (Optional) Configure **Discount combinations** to allow this offer to stack with other discounts:
    - **Product discounts**
    - **Order discounts**
    - **Shipping discounts**

![Set up discount settings](/.gitbook/assets/create-fbt/step-4.png)

14. In the **Advanced Configuration** section, configure:
    - **Schedule** — Set **Start date** and optionally toggle **Set end date** to run the offer for a limited time.
    - **Customer eligibility** — Choose **All customers** or **Specific customer segment** to target the offer.
    - **Maximum discount uses** — Toggle **Limit number of times this discount can be used in total** or **Limit to one use per customer**.
    - **Pricing option** — Toggle **Show prices per item**, **Hide option's compare-at price**, or **Use the product's compare-at price**.
15. Enter an **Offer name** in the **Offer information** section. This name is internal only — your customers will not see it.

![Configure advanced settings](/.gitbook/assets/create-fbt/step-5.png)

16. Look at the **Preview** panel on the right to see how the FBT widget will appear on your product page.
17. (Optional) Click **Next: Display setting** to customize the widget appearance before saving.
18. Click **Save** to activate the offer.

![Preview and save](/.gitbook/assets/create-fbt/step-6.png)
