# Create a Frequently Bought Together Offer

Create an FBT offer that suggests complementary products customers can buy together at a discounted price. Use AI-powered recommendations or manually select products.

<Callout type="info">
  **Prerequisites:** AOV Bundle installed. At least two products in your store.
</Callout>

## Overview

An FBT offer displays a "Frequently Bought Together" widget on product pages, showing related products with a combined bundle price and discount.

## Steps

<Steps>

### Step 1: Create a new FBT offer

Navigate to **Offers** → **Create new offer**, then click **Create** on the **Frequently Bought Together** card.

![Create FBT offer](/images/create-fbt/step-1.png)

### Step 2: Enter the offer name

In the **Offer Information** section, enter a name in the **Offer Name** field. This name is for internal use only — customers won't see this name.

![Enter offer name](/images/create-fbt/step-2.png)

### Step 3: Set trigger conditions

Configure which products or collections will trigger this FBT offer. You can target specific products, entire collections, or use AI-powered recommendations to automatically suggest related products.

![Set trigger conditions](/images/create-fbt/step-3.png)

### Step 4: Add offer products

In the **Add Offer** section, use the product browser to select the products that will be recommended alongside the trigger product. Choose products that naturally complement each other.

![Add offer products](/images/create-fbt/step-4.png)

### Step 5: Configure discount settings

In the **Discount Settings** section, select the discount type and enter the discount value. This discount applies when customers purchase the trigger product together with the recommended products.

![Configure discount](/images/create-fbt/step-5.png)

### Step 6: Customize the display

Click **Next: Display setting** to customize the FBT widget appearance on your storefront. Adjust colors, layout, and button text.

![Display settings](/images/create-fbt/step-6.png)

### Step 7: Save the offer

Click **Save** to create the offer. Activate it from the offer list to make it visible on your store.

![Save the offer](/images/create-fbt/step-7.png)

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  If you use AI recommendations, there is a maximum limit on the number of products. A banner will appear if you exceed this limit — reduce the number of selected products to continue.
</Callout>
