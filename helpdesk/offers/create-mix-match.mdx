# Create a Mix-and-Match Bundle

Create a mix-and-match bundle that lets customers pick their preferred combination of products from multiple options. Each option group has its own name and product selection.

<Callout type="info">
  **Prerequisites:** AOV Bundle installed. Products you want to include should already exist in your store.
</Callout>

## Overview

A Mix-and-Match Bundle displays multiple option groups on the product page. Customers choose one item from each group to build their personalized bundle at a discounted price. For example: "Pick a top, a bottom, and an accessory — save 15%."

## Steps

<Steps>

### Step 1: Create a new Mix-and-Match offer

Navigate to **Offers** → **Create new offer**, then click **Create** on the **Mix-and-Match Bundle** card.

![Create Mix-and-Match Bundle](/images/create-mix-match/step-1.png)

### Step 2: Enter the offer title

In the **Offer Information** section, enter a descriptive title for your bundle. This title will be visible to customers.

![Enter offer title](/images/create-mix-match/step-2.png)

### Step 3: Configure bundle options

In the **Bundle Options** section, set up each option group:
- Enter an **Option Name** (e.g., "Choose a Top", "Choose a Bottom")
- Use the product browser to add products to the option
- Click **Add bundle option** to create additional option groups
- Use the **Duplicate** or **Delete** buttons to manage options

![Configure bundle options](/images/create-mix-match/step-3.png)

### Step 4: Set discount settings

In the **Discount Settings** section, choose the discount type:
- **Fixed amount** — Discount a specific dollar amount from the bundle total
- **Fixed price** — Set a fixed price for the entire bundle

Enter the discount value in the field below.

![Set discount](/images/create-mix-match/step-4.png)

### Step 5: Customize widget content

In the **Widget Content** section, customize the text and appearance of the bundle widget that customers see on your store.

![Customize widget content](/images/create-mix-match/step-5.png)

### Step 6: Set the bundle status

In the sidebar, use the **Bundle Status** toggle to set the offer to **Active** or **Draft**. The sidebar also shows a live preview of the bundle widget.

![Set bundle status](/images/create-mix-match/step-6.png)

### Step 7: Save the bundle

Click **Save** to create the Mix-and-Match Bundle. Use **Preview on online store** or **View in Shopify** to verify how it appears to customers.

![Save the bundle](/images/create-mix-match/step-7.png)

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  You can configure a **Redirect after Add to Cart** behavior in the display settings. Choose whether customers stay on the page or go directly to the cart after adding the bundle.
</Callout>
