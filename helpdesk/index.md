## What is AOV.ai Bundle?

AOV.ai Bundle is a Shopify app that lets you create **product bundles and volume discounts** to increase your store's Average Order Value. It offers five powerful bundle types — from AI-powered product recommendations to fully customizable bundle pages — all designed to help customers buy more while feeling like they're getting a great deal.

* ✅ Products and collections ready in your Shopify store
* ✅ Clear goals in mind (e.g., clear old stock, upsell premium items, or encourage bulk buying)
