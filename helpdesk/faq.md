# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

<details>
<summary><strong>What is AOV.ai Bundle?</strong></summary>

AOV.ai Bundle is a Shopify app that helps you create product bundles and volume discounts to increase your store's Average Order Value (AOV). It supports five bundle types: Frequently Bought Together, Volume Discount, Bundle Builder, Mix-and-Match Bundle, and Product Fixed Bundle.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Is AOV.ai Bundle free to use?</strong></summary>

AOV.ai Bundle offers a free plan with basic features. Premium plans are available for stores that need advanced features like AI recommendations, priority support, and unlimited offers. Check your plan details at **AOV.ai Bundle > Settings > Subscription**.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Which Shopify plans are compatible?</strong></summary>

AOV.ai Bundle works with all Shopify plans — Basic, Shopify, Advanced, and Shopify Plus.

</details>

## Setup & Installation

<details>
<summary><strong>How do I install AOV.ai Bundle?</strong></summary>

Search for **"AOV.ai Bundle"** in the Shopify App Store, click **Add app**, then **Install app**. The app will appear under **Apps** in your Shopify admin sidebar.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Why isn't the bundle widget showing on my storefront?</strong></summary>

Make sure the **app embed** is enabled on your theme:
1. Go to **AOV.ai Bundle > Dashboard**
2. Check the **Enable app embed on theme** status — it should show **Active**
3. If not, click to enable it in your theme settings

Also verify that your offer status is set to **Active** (not Draft).

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I use AOV.ai Bundle with any Shopify theme?</strong></summary>

Yes. AOV.ai Bundle is compatible with all Shopify Online Store 2.0 themes. The widget automatically adapts to your theme. You can further customize colors, fonts, and layout in the **Widget display** settings.

</details>

## Offers & Discounts

<details>
<summary><strong>How many offers can I create?</strong></summary>

The number of offers depends on your plan. Free plans have a limited number of active offers, while premium plans allow unlimited offers. Check your plan details for the exact limits.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I combine bundle discounts with other Shopify discounts?</strong></summary>

Yes. When creating an offer, go to the **Discount settings** section and configure **Discount combinations**. You can allow combining with:
- **Product discounts**
- **Order discounts**
- **Shipping discounts**

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What discount types are available?</strong></summary>

AOV.ai Bundle supports four discount types:
- **Percentage** — Discount a percentage off (e.g., 20% off)
- **Fixed amount** — Discount a specific dollar amount (e.g., $10 off)
- **Fixed price** — Set a fixed total price for the bundle
- **Free** — Make one of the items free

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I schedule offers to run for a limited time?</strong></summary>

Yes. In the **Advanced Configuration** section of any offer, set a **Start date** and toggle **Set end date** to run the offer for a specific time period. The offer will automatically deactivate when the end date is reached.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What's the difference between Bundle Builder and Mix-and-Match?</strong></summary>

- **Bundle Builder** — You organize products into categories. Customers pick items from each category to build their bundle. Great for "Build Your Own" experiences (e.g., pick a top, bottom, and accessory).
- **Mix-and-Match** — Customers choose from predefined product options. Each option represents one selection. Great for simple "choose your items" bundles.

Both create a dedicated bundle page on your storefront.

</details>

## Widget & Display

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I customize the widget colors and text?</strong></summary>

Yes. Go to **AOV.ai Bundle > Widget display** and select the bundle type you want to customize. You can change:
- **Layout** (horizontal, vertical, card slider)
- **Color styles** (basic or advanced with individual element colors)
- **Themes** (preset color themes like Forest, Ocean Blue, etc.)
- **Content labels** (button text, saving text, translations)

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Does the widget support multiple languages?</strong></summary>

Yes. In the widget display settings, click **Translation** to add text labels in other languages. Make sure to keep dynamic variables like `{{discounted_amount}}` and `{{total_items}}` intact when translating.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I preview how the widget looks before going live?</strong></summary>

Yes. When editing an offer, the **Preview** panel on the right side shows a live preview of the widget. You can toggle between **Desktop** and **Mobile** views.

</details>
