# App Settings

Configure your AOV Bundle app settings to customize currency formats, language preferences, and integration options for your store.

<Callout type="info">
  **Prerequisites:** AOV Bundle installed with admin access to your Shopify store.
</Callout>

## Overview

Access app settings by clicking **Settings** in the app. The Settings page is available from the navigation but currently provides core configuration options for your store.

## Steps

<Steps>

### Step 1: Open Settings

Click the gear icon or navigate to the **Settings** section from within the app. The Settings page displays your current app configuration.

![Settings page](/images/app-settings/step-1.png)

### Step 2: Review store configuration

The settings page shows your current store configuration including:
- **Shop currency formats** — How prices are displayed in your offers
- **Language preferences** — The app interface language
- **Market data** — Multi-market and multi-currency settings

![Store configuration](/images/app-settings/step-2.png)

### Step 3: Configure language

Use the **Choose Language** option (also available from the dashboard top-right) to change the app interface language. This affects how the app admin UI is displayed to you.

![Configure language](/images/app-settings/step-3.png)

### Step 4: Save settings

After making changes, click **Save** to apply your updated settings.

![Save settings](/images/app-settings/step-4.png)

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  Currency format changes affect how prices are displayed in your offer widgets. Make sure the format matches your Shopify store's currency settings.
</Callout>
