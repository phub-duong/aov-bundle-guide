# Analytics Overview

The Analytics page provides a comprehensive view of your bundle offers' performance. Track revenue, conversions, and trends over time with interactive charts and data tables.

> **Info:** **Prerequisites:** AOV Bundle installed with at least one active offer that has generated data.

## Overview

Access analytics by clicking **Analytics** in the left sidebar. The page displays performance charts, trend indicators, and detailed data tables with customizable date ranges.

## Sections

### Step 1: Open the Analytics page

Click **Analytics** in the left sidebar. The analytics dashboard loads with your default date range (last 30 days).

![Analytics page](../public/images/analytics-overview/step-1.png)

### Step 2: Set the date range

Click the **date picker** control at the top of the page to select a custom date range. Choose from preset options or pick specific start and end dates. The analytics display uses your shop's timezone.

![Set date range](../public/images/analytics-overview/step-2.png)

### Step 3: Filter by offer

Use the **Offer filter** dropdown to view analytics for a specific offer type or a specific offer. Select **All Offers** to see aggregate data across all your campaigns.

![Filter by offer](../public/images/analytics-overview/step-3.png)

### Step 4: Review the line charts

The main charts show trends over time — revenue, conversions, and other key metrics. Hover over data points to see specific values. Trend indicators show whether metrics are up or down compared to the previous period.

![Revenue trend chart](../public/images/analytics-overview/step-4.png)

### Step 5: Check donut charts and sparklines

Donut charts show the distribution of metrics across offer types. Sparklines provide compact trend indicators with total values and comparison data.

![Distribution charts](../public/images/analytics-overview/step-5.png)

### Step 6: Refresh the data

Click the **Refresh** button (refresh icon) to force-reload the analytics data with the latest information from your store.

![Refresh data](../public/images/analytics-overview/step-6.png)


> **Warning:** Analytics data may take a few minutes to reflect recent orders. If you just activated a new offer, allow some time for data to populate.
