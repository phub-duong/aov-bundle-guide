# AOV.ai Bundle Help Center

Welcome to the AOV.ai Bundle documentation. Here you will find step-by-step guides for every feature of the app, from initial setup to creating advanced bundle offers.

## Documentation Sections

### [Getting Started](/docs/getting-started)
Install the app, explore the dashboard, and create your first bundle offer. This section covers everything you need to get up and running quickly.

### [Offers](/docs/offers)
Create and manage bundle offers — Volume Discounts, Frequently Bought Together, Bundle Builder, Product Fixed Bundle, and Mix-and-Match bundles.

### [Design](/docs/design)
Customize how your offer widgets look on your storefront. Choose pre-built themes, adjust colors, fonts, layouts, and auto-fit to your store's theme.

### [Analytics](/docs/analytics)
Track the performance of your bundle offers. View revenue, conversions, and trends with interactive charts and data tables.

### [Settings](/docs/settings)
Configure your app settings, manage your subscription plan, and adjust language and currency preferences.
