# How to Send an Invoice by Email

Send professional PDF invoices to your customers automatically or manually from your Shopify order page.

<Callout type="info">
  **Prerequisites:** You need at least one order in your Shopify store and an email template configured in **Settings → Email Templates**.
</Callout>

## Overview

PDF Invoice lets you email invoices directly from the order detail page. You can send to individual orders or use bulk actions for multiple orders at once.

## Steps

<Steps>

### Open the order in Shopify Admin

Navigate to **Orders** in your Shopify admin panel and click on the order you want to send an invoice for.

{/* screenshot: Shopify admin orders list with an order highlighted | page: /admin/orders | element: .order-list-item */}
![Shopify admin orders list](/images/send-invoice-email/step-1.png)

### Click "More actions" and select "Print/Send Invoice"

In the order detail page, click the **More actions** dropdown button and select **Print/Send Invoice by SUSPENDED PDF Invoice**.

{/* screenshot: More actions dropdown showing Print/Send Invoice option | page: /admin/orders/12345 | element: .more-actions-dropdown */}
![More actions dropdown with invoice option](/images/send-invoice-email/step-2.png)

### Choose "Send Email" and select your template

In the invoice dialog, select **Send Email** as the delivery method. Choose your preferred email template from the dropdown.

{/* screenshot: Invoice dialog with Send Email selected and template dropdown | page: /admin/orders/12345 | element: .invoice-dialog */}
![Invoice dialog with email option](/images/send-invoice-email/step-3.png)

### Review and send

Preview the email content, verify the recipient email address, and click **Send**. You'll see a confirmation once the email is delivered.

{/* screenshot: Email preview with Send button highlighted | page: /admin/orders/12345 | element: .email-preview */}
![Email preview before sending](/images/send-invoice-email/step-4.png)

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  Make sure your store email address is verified in **Settings → Store Details** to avoid emails landing in spam folders.
</Callout>
